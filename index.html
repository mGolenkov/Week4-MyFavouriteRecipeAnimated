Gorgeous Sandwiches with Ham and Cheese

Ingredient Amount
Bread 2 slices
Butter 2 sticks
Ham 4 slices
Cheese 4 slices
  1. Take 2 slices of fresh bread.
  2. Cut 2 sticks of butter and put it on bread.
  3. Put 2 slices of ham on each of bread pieces.
  4. Put 2 slices of cheese on each of bread pieces.
  5. Put your sandwiches in microwave for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Enjoy your meal! Do not forget to take some beer.
